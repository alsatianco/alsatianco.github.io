Cat Café Manager

Simulation

Cat Café Manager is a cozy simulation game where you build your dream cat café from scratch. Hire staff, design your interior, craft a menu, and — most importantly — rescue and care for adorable cats. Each cat has a unique personality that affects customer happiness.
